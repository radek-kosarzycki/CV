An open letter to a prospective employer

Dear Sir or Madam,

The fact that you are reading this letter means that probably I have sent out an application to your company hoping to secure a job position as a Front-end Developer. The first thing you should however know is that

I am not fit for the job that you have on offer. I am way under-qualified.

There - that is the honesty part done with.

Let me try and persuade you

why you should consider hiring me anyway

(that's some attitude, you have to admit)
